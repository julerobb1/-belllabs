About This Map

This interactive map shows the extensive AT&T Long Lines communications network that was developed throughout the 20th century. The network consisted of microwave relay towers, coaxial cable routes, and various facilities that formed the backbone of America's telecommunications infrastructure.

Use the zoom and pan controls to navigate the map. Click on individual markers to view detailed information about specific sites including historical photographs and technical specifications where available.
