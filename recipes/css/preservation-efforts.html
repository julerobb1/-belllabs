Learn about ongoing initiatives to preserve the historical legacy of Long Lines sites and Bell Labs facilities.

Bell Labs Holmdel Complex

The iconic Eero Saarinen-designed Bell Labs building in Holmdel, NJ has been redeveloped as Bell Works, a "metroburb" that preserves the architectural significance while creating new uses for the space.

Learn More

AT&T Long Lines Building (33 Thomas)

The Brutalist skyscraper at 33 Thomas Street in Manhattan, designed to house telephone switching equipment and long-distance connections, remains an architectural landmark.

Microwave Tower Documentation Project

Volunteer efforts to document and preserve the history of AT&T's microwave relay network through photography, oral histories, and site documentation.
